The first review should use synthetic or approved sample material to evaluate report structure, decision usefulness and workflow fit.

Does SpecBridge AI replace legal or commercial approval?

Bid/no-bid signals, gap visibility, clarification questions, vendor-bias notes, risk heatmap and action planning are the primary presales surfaces.
