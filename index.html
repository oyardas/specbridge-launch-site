Security and privacy

Tender and RFP documents may contain sensitive commercial, technical and legal information. SpecBridge AI pilot use requires controlled data handling, clear retention rules and explicit deletion policy.

SpecBridge AI outputs are advisory only and do not replace legal advice, final compliance approval or binding bid/no-bid decisions.
