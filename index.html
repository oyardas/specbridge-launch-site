Pilot Questions

Clear boundaries before real tender files

Can real confidential tender files be uploaded on this website?

No. The public website is a static launch surface. Real files require an agreed pilot process and data handling boundary.

What does the pilot review first?

The first review should use synthetic or approved sample material to evaluate report structure, decision usefulness and workflow fit.

Does SpecBridge AI replace legal or commercial approval?

No. It supports review preparation. Final compliance, legal, commercial and bid decisions remain human-owned.

Which outputs are useful for presales teams?

Bid/no-bid signals, gap visibility, clarification questions, vendor-bias notes, risk heatmap and action planning are the primary presales surfaces.
