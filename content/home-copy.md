## Positioning

SpecBridge AI is a Tender Intelligence and Presales Decision Support platform.

It analyzes tender, specification and RFP documents to create structured decision-support reports for sales, presales, technical and executive teams.

## Hero

Smarter tender decisions before you commit.

SpecBridge AI analyzes tender, specification and RFP documents to generate structured decision-support reports for sales, presales, technical and executive teams.

It is not only a technical compliance matrix tool. It helps teams evaluate technical fit, bid/no-bid readiness, commercial risk, legal/admin risk, vendor bias, clarification questions and action plans.

## Security / Privacy

Tender and RFP documents may contain sensitive commercial, technical and legal information.

SpecBridge AI outputs are advisory only and do not replace legal advice, final compliance approval, official procurement opinion or binding bid/no-bid decisions.
