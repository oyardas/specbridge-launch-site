## Product status

SpecBridge AI is currently a controlled demo and pilot-preparation product.

It should not be positioned as a production SaaS service until hosting, security, KVKK/legal review, data retention, file deletion and support processes are complete.

## Commercial positioning

SpecBridge AI should be positioned as:

- Tender Intelligence
- Presales Decision Support
- Compliance Strategy Assistant
- Bid / No-Bid Decision Support
- RFP and specification risk review tool

It should not be positioned as:

- Final legal advisor
- Official procurement authority
- Fully automated tender decision maker
- Guaranteed compliance engine
