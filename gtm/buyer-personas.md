# Buyer Personas

Status: Draft
Purpose: GTM messaging and demo targeting

## 1. Sales Director

### Goals

- Decide quickly whether to pursue a tender
- Understand commercial and competitive risks
- Avoid wasting presales capacity
- Prepare management-ready opportunity summaries

### Pain points

- Late-stage no-bid decisions
- Unclear competitive position
- Manual coordination across teams
- Weak visibility into tender risks

### Message

SpecBridge AI gives sales leadership a structured bid/no-bid surface before the team spends excessive time on the response.

## 2. Presales Engineer

### Goals

- Build compliance matrix faster
- Identify missing technical requirements
- Generate clarification questions
- Map requirements to products and evidence

### Pain points

- Manual clause extraction
- Ambiguous technical requirements
- Repetitive report preparation
- Last-minute sales pressure

### Message

SpecBridge AI reduces manual tender review effort and turns document clauses into structured technical review outputs.

## 3. Bid Manager

### Goals

- Standardize tender response workflow
- Track risks and missing information
- Coordinate sales, technical, legal and operations inputs
- Create consistent report packages

### Pain points

- Inconsistent input from teams
- Missing administrative requirements
- Late clarification questions
- Poor audit trail

### Message

SpecBridge AI creates a repeatable tender intelligence package across executive, technical and sales/presales views.

## 4. General Manager / Country Manager

### Goals

- See executive-level risk and opportunity score
- Prioritize resources
- Avoid high-risk tenders
- Improve forecast quality

### Pain points

- Over-optimistic opportunity qualification
- Hidden delivery or legal risks
- Lack of standardized decision evidence

### Message

SpecBridge AI gives management a concise decision-support layer for tender participation.
