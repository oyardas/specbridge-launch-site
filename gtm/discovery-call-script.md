# Discovery Call Script

Status: Draft
Purpose: First customer discovery conversation

## 1. Opening

We are preparing SpecBridge AI, a Tender Intelligence and Presales Decision Support platform for teams that review RFPs, tenders and technical specifications.

The goal is to understand your current tender review process and see whether a controlled pilot would be useful.

## 2. Current workflow questions

1. How many tender/RFP documents do you review per month?
2. Which teams participate in the review?
3. Who owns bid/no-bid decisions?
4. How is the compliance matrix prepared today?
5. How are legal, commercial and delivery risks captured?
6. How are clarification questions created?
7. Which part of the process takes the most time?

## 3. Pain discovery

1. Where do tenders usually fail late?
2. Which risks are discovered too late?
3. Do you face vendor-biased specifications?
4. Do you need different report formats for management and technical teams?
5. How often do teams disagree on bid/no-bid decisions?

## 4. Product fit questions

1. Would a structured executive summary be useful?
2. Would automated clause extraction and compliance matrix preparation save time?
3. Would vendor bias detection help?
4. Would clarification question generation be useful?
5. Which export format matters most: HTML, DOCX, PDF, XLSX or JSON?

## 5. Security and data handling

1. Can you use anonymized historical tenders for a pilot?
2. Are there documents that cannot leave your environment?
3. Would local/private deployment be required?
4. What approval is needed before testing real documents?
5. What deletion confirmation would you require?

## 6. Close

Suggested next step:

Run one anonymized scenario through SpecBridge AI, review the output together and score usefulness across executive, technical and sales/presales views.
