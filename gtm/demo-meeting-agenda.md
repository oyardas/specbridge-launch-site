Purpose: 30-minute pilot demo agenda

- Suitable anonymized document
- Security/legal constraints
- Preferred report formats
- Success criteria

## 25 - 30 min: Next steps

- Confirm pilot owner
- Agree sample document boundary
- Define deletion expectation
- Schedule output review session
- Collect feedback form
