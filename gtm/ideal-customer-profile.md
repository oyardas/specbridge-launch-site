# Ideal Customer Profile

Status: Draft
Purpose: Early GTM targeting

## Primary ICP

Organizations that frequently review technical tenders, RFPs and specifications and need to decide whether to bid, how to respond and where the risks are.

## Best-fit company types

- IT system integrators
- Network and infrastructure vendors
- Value-added resellers
- Distributor presales teams
- Cybersecurity solution providers
- Data center / HCI / cloud infrastructure providers
- Public sector tender response teams
- Enterprise procurement support teams

## Strong qualification signals

- Reviews more than 5 tenders/RFPs per month
- Uses manual compliance matrix preparation
- Has separate sales, presales and bid teams
- Frequently faces vendor-biased specifications
- Needs management-level bid/no-bid summaries
- Works with public-sector or enterprise procurement
- Must create repeatable report packages

## Weak-fit signals

- Very low tender volume
- Purely transactional small quotes
- No formal presales process
- No need for compliance documentation
- No willingness to upload or share documents, even anonymized
- Expects fully automated legal approval

## Priority verticals

1. IT infrastructure
2. Network and wireless
3. Data center and HCI
4. Cybersecurity
5. Enterprise software
6. Public-sector technology tenders
7. Smart campus / smart building technology

## Early pilot target

The first pilot should be with a team that can provide anonymized tender examples and structured feedback without requiring immediate production hosting.
