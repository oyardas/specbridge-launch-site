# Pilot Success Criteria

Status: Draft
Purpose: Define how early pilots will be judged

## 1. Time saving

Target signal:

- Customer says output reduces manual review effort
- Presales can reuse compliance matrix or gap list
- Management can use executive summary without manual rewriting

## 2. Risk visibility

Target signal:

- Commercial, legal/admin, delivery and SLA risks are visible earlier
- Vendor bias findings are useful
- Clarification questions are actionable

## 3. Report usefulness

Target signal:

- Executive report is useful for management
- Technical report is useful for presales
- Sales/presales report is useful for opportunity strategy
- Export formats match workflow needs

## 4. Trust

Target signal:

- Customer understands output is advisory
- Customer is willing to test with anonymized real scenario
- Customer does not require unsupported production claims

## 5. Buying signal

Target signal:

- Customer asks about paid pilot
- Customer asks about private/local deployment
- Customer wants to compare outputs against internal process
- Customer identifies owner and next meeting

## 6. No-go signals

- Customer expects legal guarantee
- Customer refuses any document sharing or anonymization
- Tender volume is too low
- No clear internal owner
- Security requirements exceed current readiness
