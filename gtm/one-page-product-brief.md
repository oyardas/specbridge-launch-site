# One-Page Product Brief

Status: Draft
Purpose: Product brief for early pilot and website use

## Product name

SpecBridge AI

## One-line description

SpecBridge AI helps sales, presales and management teams analyze tender, RFP and specification documents and convert them into structured decision-support reports.

## Problem

Tender review is often slow, manual and fragmented across sales, presales, technical, legal and operations teams.

Common issues:

- Late visibility of commercial and legal risks
- Manual compliance matrix preparation
- Unclear bid/no-bid rationale
- Vendor-biased requirements
- Missing clarification questions
- Inconsistent report format across teams
- Weak executive decision surface

## Solution

SpecBridge AI analyzes tender/RFP documents and generates structured outputs such as:

- Executive Summary
- Tender / Document Classification
- Bid / No-Bid Recommendation
- Overall Scores
- Technical Fit
- Clause Compliance Matrix
- Gap Analysis
- Vendor Bias / Lock-in Analysis
- Rewrite / Objection Suggestions
- Evidence / Product Mapping
- Clarification Questions
- Commercial Risk
- Legal / Administrative Risk
- Delivery / Installation / Acceptance Risk
- SLA / Warranty / Support Review
- BOM / Product Mapping
- Risk Heatmap
- Action Plan
- Appendix / Audit Trail

## Target users

- Sales managers
- Presales engineers
- Bid managers
- Solution architects
- Channel teams
- System integrators
- Vendors responding to public or private tenders

## Main value

SpecBridge AI helps teams reduce review time, standardize bid evaluation and identify risks before committing technical and commercial resources.

## Current status

Controlled local/demo-ready MVP.

External AI, DB read and live evidence lookup are not active in the current controlled mode.

## Disclaimer

Outputs are advisory decision-support materials. They do not replace legal advice, final compliance approval, procurement authority review or binding bid/no-bid governance.
