# Privacy Policy Draft

Status: Draft
Purpose: SpecBridge AI launch readiness and pilot preparation
Review required: Legal counsel / KVKK advisor

## 1. Scope

This Privacy Policy draft explains how personal data and document-related information may be handled during the controlled demo and pilot preparation stage of SpecBridge AI.

SpecBridge AI is not yet positioned as a production SaaS service. The current website and pilot interest form are static/mockup unless a backend, consent mechanism and data handling process are explicitly activated.

## 2. Data Categories

Potential data categories may include:

- Contact data: name, surname, email, company, role
- Company information: organization name, business context, tender volume
- Pilot interest data: use case, message, feedback
- Document metadata: file name, document type, upload timestamp, analysis package reference
- Tender/RFP content: only if explicitly provided for controlled pilot or demo testing

## 3. Sensitive Tender Documents

Tender, specification and RFP documents may contain sensitive commercial, technical, contractual or legal information.

Real customer documents must not be uploaded or processed before:

- Explicit customer approval
- Privacy/KVKK notice
- Data retention policy
- File deletion policy
- Security review
- Pilot disclaimer acceptance

## 4. Processing Purposes

Data may be processed for:

- Responding to pilot/demo interest
- Understanding relevant use cases
- Preparing controlled demo sessions
- Improving sample report structure
- Collecting pilot feedback
- Managing security and deletion requests

## 5. Data Sharing

Data must not be shared with third parties unless required for:

- Legal obligation
- Security operations
- Hosting or infrastructure providers
- Explicitly approved pilot support

Any third-party processor must be reviewed separately before production use.

## 6. Retention

Pilot interest data and demo-related files must follow the separate Data Retention Policy.

Real tender documents should be deleted after the agreed pilot review window unless a written exception exists.

## 7. Advisory Output Only

SpecBridge AI outputs are advisory decision-support materials.

They do not constitute:

- Legal advice
- Final compliance approval
- Binding bid/no-bid decision
- Official procurement opinion
- Customer-specific contractual advice

## 8. Review Status

This document is a draft and must not be published as final policy before legal/KVKK review.
