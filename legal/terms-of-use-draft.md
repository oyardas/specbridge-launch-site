# Terms of Use Draft

Status: Draft
Purpose: SpecBridge AI launch readiness and pilot preparation
Review required: Legal counsel / KVKK advisor

## 1. Scope

These Terms of Use apply to the controlled demo, pilot preparation website and sample materials of SpecBridge AI.

SpecBridge AI is currently a local/demo-ready MVP approaching pilot readiness. It is not yet a production SaaS service.

## 2. Permitted Use

SpecBridge AI materials may be used for:

- Product evaluation
- Demo review
- Pilot discussion
- Sample report assessment
- Internal decision-support workflow evaluation

## 3. Prohibited Use

Users must not:

- Upload confidential customer documents without authorization
- Upload personal data or sensitive data without a valid legal basis
- Treat outputs as final legal or procurement advice
- Use sample reports as binding compliance approval
- Reverse engineer, copy or redistribute private pilot materials without permission

## 4. Document Upload and Processing

Real tender/RFP documents may only be used in a controlled pilot scope.

Before using real customer documents, the following must be defined:

- Customer approval
- Data retention period
- File deletion process
- Security responsibility
- Output disclaimer
- Access control model

## 5. Advisory Nature of Output

SpecBridge AI outputs are advisory only.

Outputs do not replace:

- Legal counsel
- Contract review
- Procurement authority decision
- Technical sign-off
- Commercial approval
- Final bid/no-bid governance

## 6. No Warranty

Demo and pilot materials are provided as-is for evaluation purposes.

No warranty is given that the analysis is complete, error-free, legally sufficient or suitable for final tender submission.

## 7. Liability Limitation

Any use of the output for commercial, legal, technical or procurement decisions remains the responsibility of the user organization.

## 8. Review Status

This document is a draft and must not be published as final Terms of Use before legal review.
