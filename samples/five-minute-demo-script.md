# Five-Minute Demo Script

Status: Draft
Purpose: Controlled pilot demo flow
Data type: Synthetic / anonymized

## 0:00 - 0:30 Opening

SpecBridge AI is a Tender Intelligence and Presales Decision Support platform.

It helps teams review tender, RFP and specification documents beyond a simple technical compliance matrix.

## 0:30 - 1:00 Problem

Tender review is usually fragmented across sales, presales, technical, legal and operations teams.

This creates late risk visibility, inconsistent bid/no-bid decisions and manual reporting overhead.

## 1:00 - 1:45 Product flow

The workflow is simple:

1. Upload the tender document
2. Analyze technical, commercial and administrative content
3. Review the decision surface
4. Download executive, technical and sales/presales report packages

## 1:45 - 2:45 Decision surface

The decision surface shows:

- Bid/no-bid signal
- Technical fit
- Compliance matrix
- Gap list
- Vendor bias
- Clarification questions
- Commercial and delivery risks
- SLA/warranty/support review
- Action plan

## 2:45 - 3:30 Report profiles

SpecBridge AI can generate different outputs for different stakeholders:

- Executive report for management decision
- Technical report for solution and compliance review
- Sales/presales report for opportunity strategy

## 3:30 - 4:15 Vendor bias example

The system can flag vendor-specific language and suggest neutral rewrite options.

This helps teams prepare clarification questions and protect bid eligibility.

## 4:15 - 4:45 Security and pilot note

This demo uses synthetic or anonymized data only.

Real tender documents require explicit approval, retention rules, file deletion process and legal/KVKK readiness.

## 4:45 - 5:00 Close

The goal of the pilot is to validate whether SpecBridge AI reduces review time, improves risk visibility and standardizes bid decision support.

Suggested next step: review one anonymized scenario together and collect structured feedback.
