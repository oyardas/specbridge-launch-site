# Vendor Bias Before / After Example

Status: Draft
Purpose: Demonstrate vendor bias detection and neutral rewrite
Data type: Synthetic / anonymized

## 1. Original biased clause

The wireless system shall support Magic-style onboarding and single-pane campus control similar to Vendor X architecture.

## 2. Detected issue

This clause may create vendor bias because it references a vendor-specific architecture or terminology.

Risk:

- Competing vendors may object
- Equivalent solutions may be unfairly excluded
- Bid evaluation may become less neutral
- Procurement process may face clarification or objection requests

## 3. Neutral rewrite suggestion

The wireless system shall support centralized onboarding, configuration, monitoring and policy management through a unified network management interface. Equivalent architectures that provide the same operational outcomes shall be accepted.

## 4. Alternative stronger neutral rewrite

The proposed wireless solution shall provide centralized lifecycle management, including device onboarding, configuration deployment, monitoring, event visibility, inventory and policy control. The requirement shall be evaluated based on functional equivalence rather than vendor-specific terminology.

## 5. Suggested clarification question

Can the buyer confirm that vendor-specific terminology in the wireless management requirement is illustrative and that functionally equivalent solutions from other vendors will be accepted?

## 6. Sales / Presales note

This finding should be handled carefully. The goal is not to accuse the buyer of bias, but to protect bid eligibility and create a neutral evaluation basis.

## 7. Disclaimer

This example is synthetic and for demonstration purposes only.
