# Bid / No-Bid Decision Card

Status: Public sample
Product: SpecBridge AI
Data type: Synthetic / anonymized

## Recommendation

Conditional Bid.

## Reasoning

The synthetic RFP appears technically addressable, but final bid readiness depends on clarifying vendor-specific wording, acceptance criteria, delivery responsibilities and support obligations.

## Proceed if

- Functional equivalence is accepted.
- Acceptance criteria are defined.
- SLA and warranty expectations are confirmed.
- Submission format is clarified.
- Delivery dependencies are agreed.

## Do not proceed if

- Vendor-specific wording remains mandatory.
- Acceptance remains undefined.
- Commercial obligations cannot be bounded.
- Support terms create open-ended liability.
