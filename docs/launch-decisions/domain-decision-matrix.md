# Domain Decision Matrix

Status: Draft
Decision owner: Önder Yardaş
Scope: SpecBridge AI public launch

## Domain strategy

SpecBridge AI should use a domain that signals B2B trust, tender/RFP intelligence, and AI-enabled decision support. The domain should be short, pronounceable, defensible, and suitable for international use.

## Availability status

Availability is not verified in this repository operation. Final availability must be checked directly at the registrar immediately before purchase.

## Shortlist

| Rank | Domain | Use case | Strength | Concern | Recommendation |
|---:|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | specbridge.ai | Primary product domain | Best product-market fit for AI positioning | Premium cost or unavailable risk | Buy if available |
| 2 | specbridge.co | Primary fallback | Short, international, credible | Less AI-specific | Buy if .ai unavailable |
| 3 | specbridge.app | Product/app positioning | Suitable if product access later becomes app-based | Slightly less enterprise feel | Reserve if affordable |
| 4 | specbridge.tech | Technical positioning | Clear technology signal | More generic | Fallback only |
| 5 | specbridge.com.tr | Türkiye credibility | Useful for local trust and Turkish pilots | Localized scope | Defensive/local purchase if available |
| 6 | getspecbridge.ai | Fallback if exact .ai unavailable | Still clear and action-oriented | Longer | Use only if exact name unavailable |
| 7 | usespecbridge.com | Broad fallback | Useful for marketing | Less premium | Last fallback |
| 8 | specbridgecloud.com | Cloud/product angle | Available-style fallback | Less clean brand | Avoid unless needed |

## Scoring model

| Criterion | Weight | Notes |
|---|---:|---|
| Brand clarity | 25 | Should clearly support the SpecBridge name |
| B2B credibility | 20 | Must be acceptable for enterprise and public-sector conversations |
| AI/product relevance | 15 | Should support tender intelligence positioning |
| Pronunciation and memorability | 15 | Should be easy to say in Turkish and English |
| Legal/trademark risk | 10 | Must be checked before launch |
| Expansion fit | 10 | Should work beyond one country or one vertical |
| SEO ambiguity | 5 | Lower ambiguity is better |

## Preferred decision

Primary target: specbridge.ai

Defensive reservations if budget allows:

- specbridge.co
- specbridge.com.tr
- specbridge.app

## Domain purchase pre-check

Before purchase:

1. Registrar availability check
2. Trademark search
3. Similar company/product search
4. Social handle search
5. GitHub/org naming check
6. Email deliverability check
7. Renewal price check
8. WHOIS privacy availability check
9. DNSSEC and MFA support check
10. Invoice/company ownership check

## Naming rules

Avoid:

- Hyphenated domains
- Long phrase domains
- Domains that imply production SaaS before the product is production-ready
- Domains that overpromise automated legal/commercial decision-making
- Domains that include vendor names
